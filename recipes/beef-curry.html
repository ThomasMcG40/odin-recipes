Beef Curry Recipe

Home

This Japanese style beef curry will have your mouth watering.

Authentic Japanese curry is quite the treat! Enjoy it at home quickly and easily with this Japanese curry recipe.

You can easily get a block of Japanese curry and take a lot of the workload off of yourself with this recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large yukon gold potatoes
  • 2 Medium sized carrots
  • 1 Large onion
  • 1 Lb beef chuck
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 8 Cups water
  • 12oz Japanese curry block
  • Warm cooked rice to serve with

Instructions:

  1. Prepare meat and vegatables: Peel potato, carrot and onion then cut into large chunks. Cut beef into same size as the vegetables.
  2. Cook beef: Heat a large pot over hight heat and add cooking oil. Add the beef and season with salt and pepper. Sear the beef all sides, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Add the vegatables: Add the vegetables and sauté for 2 to 3 minutes. Add the water and stir well. Bring it to boil and hard boil for 6 to 8 minutes or until the potato is fully cooked. Skim the scums as needed.
  4. Add curry block: Add curry blocks and reduce heat to medium low. Stir well until the curry blocks are completely dissolved. Simmer for 1 more minute and it’s ready to serve!
  5. Serve: Serve with warm cooked rice is classic, fried or poached egg is an optional to your taste! Enjoy!
